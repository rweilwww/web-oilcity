Oil City Trail - Amenities Overview



Rest rooms, water: Seasonal rest rooms at the PA Fish and Boat Commission access. and Justus Park

Bike shop, rentals: none.

Restaurant, groceries: Several restaurants, stores in Oil City.

Camping, simple lodging: Several motels/hotels in Oil City.

Swimming, fishing: The Allegheny River has strong currents and power boats.

Winter sports: none.

Wheelchair access: On roads, some sidewalk.

Summary Rest rooms, water, food, lodging, fishing


Amenities are organized by major trailheads. These trailheads are listed in order from the start of the trail to the end of the trail. In the trailhead listing the amenities are grouped around the nearest access place, and listed in the order of those closest to the trailhead to those farthest from the trailhead.
